Volumetric Weight Calculation

How to Calculate Volumetric Weight

To calculate the volumetric weight of your shipment, simply multiply the length (cm) x height (cm) x width (cm) and divide the result by 5,000.

If you're sending multiple packages, calculate each weight separately and this is the 'total chargeable weight'. The chargeable weight is always the greater of the actual or volumetric weight.


The cost of a shipment can be affected by the amount of space that it occupies on an aircraft, rather than the actual weight. This is the volumetric weight. Chargeable weight may be either volumetric weight or gross weight, whichever is greater.
